About Us

Falcon Tech BD is a Startup company supported by ICT Division, Bangladesh.

We are working on award-winning Innovation AgroDrone project, an autonomous drone-based data collection and analytics platform for increasing crop productivity and predicting market demand.

We have the solution for eliminating problems which are holding back the true potential of your Agriculture.

  • Get the proper estimation of how much crop you will get
  • Suggestion on which Crop Should you cultivate
  • Get to know when and which pesticide you should use
  • Get updates about Your crops in real time

We are committed to get you the information

From data collected in our routine Drone flight, we gather lots of information about your crops.

  • 01How can this informations help?

    You can take a look a our data and plan your next move. A pest or disease may harm your crops if you don't take care of it. You can know about such things and make your crops most profitable.

  • 02 How our yield prediction is so accurate?

    Our prediction is based on machine learning. We have collected a lot of data. We combine this data with other resources that correlate with the production. Thus, we get highly accurate prediction.
